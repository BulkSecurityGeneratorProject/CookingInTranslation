It begins...

in the kitchen, where all great food comes alive. With smiles drawn, dinner was ready.

I grew up in a multi-lingual household; sometimes, we don't soak everything in. Sometimes, it gets lost in translation...

I created this site as a way to connect with my mother. Despite my efforts to truly understand her language, there were just some things that only she could pull off in the kitchen, no matter how much google translate tried, there wasn't a word for what she was doing.

Cooking in translation is my tool to store the recipes my mother wants me to have, in a language I can understand. My own.
